Assistant Professor of Finance

Stephen M. Ross School of Business, University of Michigan

I am an Assistant Professor of Finance at the University of Michigan, Ross School of Business. I received my Ph.D. from Copenhagen Business School in 2025. My research is in asset pricing, financial intermediaries, and derivatives, studying how intermediary frictions and market design shape asset prices, risk sharing, and real investment.
